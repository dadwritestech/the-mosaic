# The Mosaic — Worldbuilding Bible

**Date:** 2026-06-07
**Status:** Locked (v1) — basis for sub-project 4 content.
**Authors:** 3-way jam — user (direction), Claude (engine-aware synthesis), local `pi` (concept generation).

> Creative reference for the region. Mechanics live in the sub-project specs; this is
> the "what the world IS" source of truth.

## Premise

*The Mosaic* — fragments of every classic region fused around a living **World Core**.
The convergence is a recurring cosmic **cycle** that has happened before and *failed*,
leaving "branching scars." The world is half-real, half-**memory** sustained by the
Core — and it has **reset before.**

## Box Legendary

**Convergeon, the Soul-Weaver** — embodies the collective memory of every trainer who
ever walked the path. (Rhymes with the game's adaptive AI that remembers the player.)

## Signature mechanics (engine-cheap by design)

- **Convergence Tide** — a route's wild dex shifts by day/time (uses the clock +
  `timeOfDay` from 3d).
- **Stabilize ↔ Destabilize meter** — player choices reshape encounters, difficulty,
  and the ending.
- **Reality Glitches** — eerie artifacts hinting at "previous cycles" (the save/memory
  system made diegetic).

## Story spine & twists

1. The convergence destabilizes; regions bleed, Pokémon mutate. The **Elite Four**
   secretly debate **Reset** (split regions back) vs. **Embrace** (one unified world).
2. **Twist:** it has happened before — the world resets repeatedly; the player may be
   a memory-wiped past Champion (the save/memory system = the world remembering you).
3. **Twist:** Resetting would erase the player's own convergence-born Pokémon — the
   final choice costs everything.
4. **Twist:** some "wild" Pokémon are displaced citizens of erased towns.

## Factions

- **Purists** — force a Reset, even at the cost of the new life.
- **Synthesists** — force a total merge into one species/world.
- **Archivists** — manipulate from the shadows, hoarding old-world knowledge.

## Elite Four → Champion

- **Ferrum** (Steel) — unyielding industrialist, "forge the world anew."
- **Lumina** (Fairy) — sweet-voiced enchantress, ruthless agenda.
- **Wraith** (Ghost) — remembers every past cycle; **the most adaptive opponent.**
- **Kairos** (Fighting) — disciplined monk, seeks to "break" the cycle by will.
- **Champion: the Warden** — neutral arbiter fielding one legendary per era; tests
  whether the player is worthy to choose the world's fate (not just to win).
